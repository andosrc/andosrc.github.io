LadyFest Tallinn 2016 presents: Welcome me Estonia. Добро пожаловать мне в Эстонии. Tere tulemast mulle Eestis.

Participants: Ahmed from Egypt, Irina from Ukraine, Maret from Ingushetia, Olga and Mira from Ukraine, Qeti from Georgia, Sattar Ilyas from Balochistan, Sonia from Dagestan, Zahra from Afghanistan and Umar Saleem from Pakistan
The works at the exhibition are made by the asylum seekers in Estonia. The LadyFest team organised art workshops at the Vao refugee centre from November 2015 to March 2016.
LadyFest Tallinn 2016 team: Minna Hint, Mari-Leen Kiipli, Pire Sova and Killu Sukmit, Anna Stina Treumund
