Post-Summer Choir

Johhan Rosenberg
Published in A Shade Colder magazine

Stage and costume design
Von Krahl Theatre, Tallinn, Rakvere and Kaunas
2024

The characters in Post-Summer Choir are the trapped souls of the Estonian past. They mistake beauty for ugliness and ugliness for beauty. Over the course of the performance, costumes unravel layer by layer, as do the intergenerational fossils of emotions.
